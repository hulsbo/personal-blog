    Cattegat cruise day 1: Since long time, and especially since the start of my residence in Goteborg, I've wanted to try to cover the distances along the west coast of Sweden I usually do by train instead on my bike. With a nice weather report and with newly bought shoes this weekend felt like a good opportunity for realizing that wish. So I took Friday off from work and went off with Haverdal (summerhouse there) as my first stop, with Hoganas being my final destination. Except from a bit of trouble finding an effective way (the cycle road I followed were meant to be beautiful in first hand), some struggle with clipping shoes off/on, and also an inferior bike light for august nights, it went well. Total time riding (just riding) was 7h 30m. With break time in addition to that total travelling amounted to 10 hours 20 minutes. Pedaled my last kilometer 10 minutes after midnight. In total 167 km. Should have started earlier, but at least now I know the time required for doing this type of thing.
